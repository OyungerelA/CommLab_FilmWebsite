Idea

Since we had quite a trouble trying to decide on a topic, our film depicts an almost alternate reality where everything goes smoothly and we are able to come up with an idea rather quickly. This film idea was first initiated by our teammate, Minnie Mouse, and our group as a whole all liked it and decided to continue with it.

Filming Process

Our filming took about 15 minutes and we recorded it using Zoom. We spent another 10-15 minutes searching for and adding the background song using iMovie.

Outcome

Although filming and editing a film in half an hour was a little stressful, it was a great experience overall and we had a lot of fun making our movie. We could not include everything we planned because of the time constraints, but sharing our film with our class and watching other films of our classmates' was super enjoyable. At the end of the day, it is the experience that matters, not the outcome :)

About Us

We are a group of four students studying at NYU Abu Dhabi and we made this film as part of our summer course, Communications Lab, taught by professor Pierre Depaz. This is our entry to the annual 30-Minute Film Festival 2021.
